### 6️⃣ Check Leap Year
**Leap year condition:**  
- `year % 400 == 0` OR  
- `year % 4 == 0 AND year % 100 != 0`

**Example**  
Input: `2024`  
Output: `Leap Year`

### 🔟 Reverse a Number
**Example**  
Input: `1234`  
Output: `4321`  

**Logic:**  
digit = n % 10
reverse = reverse * 10 + digit
n = n / 10
